About Dignity for Children Foundation

Learn about our mission, vision, and 25 years of impact in Sentul, Kuala Lumpur.

Our Mission

To break the cycle of poverty through education by providing holistic development opportunities to underprivileged children and youth in the Sentul community — empowering them to transform their lives and communities.

Our Vision

A future where every child, regardless of socio-economic background, has access to quality education and the opportunity to reach their full potential while contributing meaningfully to society.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Dignity for Children Foundation began as a small learning center responding to the urgent need for quality education among the urban poor in Sentul, Kuala Lumpur. What started with just 15 children has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving over 1,500 students annually.

Our founders believed education is the most powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. Situated in one of Kuala Lumpur’s most densely populated low-income areas, Dignity’s approach integrates academics with character building, life skills, and vocational training.

Today, we continue to expand our reach — providing dignity, hope, and opportunity to every child who walks through our doors.
