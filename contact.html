Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know my donation is used effectively?

+

We publish regular reports and updates. Over 85% of all donations go directly to programmes. Independent audits ensure transparency and accountability.

Can I visit the foundation and meet the children?

+

Yes! We welcome visitors and supporters. Please contact us beforehand to schedule a guided tour.

What volunteer opportunities are available?

+

We offer tutoring, mentoring, administration, and skills-based volunteering. Reach out to see how you can help.

Are donations tax-deductible in Malaysia?

+

Yes, Dignity for Children Foundation is a registered NGO in Malaysia, and eligible donations are tax-deductible.
